Iraqi Brayni

Prep : 30m

Cook : 30m

Difficulty : 5\3.5

What Makes Iraqi Bryani So Special ?

Rice-based: Usually long-grain rice like basmati is used. Meat: Chicken, beef, or lamb, often cooked with a mix of aromatic spices. Spices: Unlike Indian biryani, Iraqi biryani is less spicy but more aromatic. Common spices include cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, nutmeg, and black lime (loomi). Nuts & raisins: Cashews or almonds and raisins are often added for a subtle sweetness. Tomato sauce: Sometimes the meat is cooked in a light tomato-based sauce before mixing with rice. Garnish: Fried onions, chopped parsley.

Cooking Style

The meat and rice are usually cooked separately. Rice is often cooked with saffron or turmeric to give it a golden color. After that, the meat is layered with the rice and allowed to steam together so the flavors meld and its usually served with salad and yogurt

How To Make Iraqi Bryani?

    Ingredients :

  1. cups of long-grain rice (Basmati preferred)
  2. 4 cups of water
  3. 1/4 tsp saffron
  4. 1 tsp of olive oil
  5. 1 medium onions,chopped
  6. 2 cloves of garlic,minced
  7. 2 tsp of ground cinnamon
  8. 3 large potatos,minced
  9. 1 tsp of turmeric
  10. 2 tsp of salt

Instructions :

1. Wash the rice well until water runs clear, soak for 20-30m, then drain

2. Cook the chicken by heating the oil in a largre pot saute onions until it becomes golden,add the garlic,then chicken pieces,cook until lightly browned

3. Add the spices and cook for 5-7m untilthe chicken is coated

4. Cook the rice with the chicken and add drained rice to the pot, stir gently to mix it with the chicken and spices,pour in water or chicken broth,bring to a boil

Tips :

1. Dont stir the rice too much while cooking -- it can break and become mushy

2. You can add a pinch of saffron soaked in warm water for extra aroma

3. You can make it better by adding carrots for a richer bryani
